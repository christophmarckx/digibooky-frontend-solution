Story 4: Search a book by title

As a user I want to search for a book by entering a part of the title, so I can efficiently find a specific book.

  • I should be able to enter wildcards if I don't know the complete title.

How to test?

Used endpoint: GET …/books?title=…

  • Add a couple of books to your list of books.
  • Click on the following link or on the "BOOKS" link at the top of your screen, this will send you to the Book Overview Page.
  • Here you can fill in the part of your title that you are looking for. The title should only match just a part of the title and not the whole title.
  • If everything works Fine, you should see a list of your books with the asked title.

If something went wrong, possible solutions.

  • Make sure that you check the title correctly.
