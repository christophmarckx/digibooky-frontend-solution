How to test Story 11:

  • Login with an Librarian account or use the special librarian login on the following link:
  • Because you are logged in with an account, but not with an librarian, you should click following link:
    Next, you should login with an librarian account.
  • Click on the following link or on the "BOOKS" link at the top of your screen, this will send you to the Book Overview Page.
  • Here you can choose a book that you would like to borrow from DigiBooky by clicking on the "Detail" button
  • Now you should be on the details page from your chosen book.
  • On this page you should see the "Lent Book" button.
  • when you press this button, you should have borrowed the book.
  • When the Profile story is already implemented, you can see the chosen book on the profile page.

If something went wrong:

  • The "Lent Book" button isn't available on the book details page

    • Make sure you followed the above steps and you are really logged in.

      If you are sure you are logged in, check if your role is "member" and not something else

      to check this:

      • Press the right mouse button and select "inspect" (normally the last option you can choose)
      • Now you should see that there is a box popping up in your browser, at the top of this box there are multiple options, one of them should be "Application"
      • When you press on "Application", you see a "Storage" section
      • In this section is the option "Session Storage", click on its the drop down button
      • In this dropdown, should be the "http://localhost:4200" option, click on this
      • When you clicked on this, you should see "Key" and "Value"
      • Look under "Key" for the name "role" and check if that corresponding "Value" is "member"
