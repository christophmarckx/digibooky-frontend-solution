How to test Story 3:

  • Add a couple of books to your list of books.
  • Click on the following link or on the "BOOKS" link at the top of your screen, this will send you to the Book Overview Page.
  • Here you can fill in the part of your ISBN that you are looking for. The ISBN should only match just a part of the ISBN and not the whole ISBN.
  • If everything works Fine, you should see a list of your books with the asked ISBN number.

If something went wrong, possible solutions.

  • Make sure that you check the ISBN correctly... Did you also think about the "-" character?
